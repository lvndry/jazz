# Average American Weekly Shopping Cart (typical 1–2 person household)

This is a representative weekly shopping cart for an average American household, grouped by category with typical quantities. Adjust quantities up or down for larger households.

## Produce
- Bananas — 6
- Apples — 4
- Oranges / clementines — 4
- Grapes — 1 lb
- Strawberries or mixed berries — 1 pint
- Mixed salad greens or romaine — 5–10 oz bag or 1 head
- Tomatoes — 3–4
- Onions — 2 lb
- Potatoes — 5 lb
- Carrots — 1 lb
- Broccoli — 1–2 heads

## Dairy & Eggs
- Milk (whole or 2%) — 1 gallon
- Eggs — 1 dozen
- Butter — 1 lb (or stick pack)
- Cheddar cheese — 8 oz
- Shredded mozzarella — 8 oz
- Yogurt — 4-pack or 32 oz tub

## Meat & Protein
- Chicken breasts or thighs — 2–3 lb
- Ground beef or turkey — 1 lb
- Bacon — 12 oz
- Deli turkey or ham — 8–12 oz
- Canned tuna — 2 cans
- Tofu — 14 oz (optional)

## Bakery & Grains
- Sandwich bread (whole wheat or white) — 1 loaf
- Tortillas — 1 pack
- Rice — 2–5 lb bag
- Pasta — 1 lb
- Oats — 18 oz canister
- English muffins or bagels — 1 pack

## Pantry & Canned Goods
- Canned beans (black, pinto, or chickpeas) — 2–4 cans
- Canned tomatoes / pasta sauce — 2 jars or cans
- Chicken or vegetable broth — 1 carton
- Peanut butter — 16 oz
- Jam / jelly — 1 jar
- Cooking oil (olive or vegetable) — 1 bottle
- All-purpose flour — 2–5 lb (as needed)
- Sugar — 2–4 lb (as needed)
- Salt & pepper — as needed
- Common spices (e.g., garlic powder, Italian seasoning) — small containers
- Coffee — 12–16 oz
- Tea — 1 box

## Frozen
- Frozen mixed vegetables — 1 bag
- Frozen fruit (berries) — 1 bag
- Frozen pizza or ready meal — 1–2
- Ice cream — 1 pint

## Beverages
- Bottled water or large jug — 1
- Orange juice — 1 container
- Soda — 2-liter bottle or 6-pack
- (Optional) Beer or wine — 1–6 pack/bottle

## Snacks & Treats
- Potato chips — 1 bag
- Nuts (salted or unsalted) — 8–12 oz
- Granola or protein bars — 4–6 bars
- Cookies or sweet snack — 1 package
- Chocolate or candy — small pack

## Household & Personal Care
- Toilet paper — typical pack (4–12 rolls)
- Paper towels — 1–2 rolls
- Dish soap — 1 bottle
- Laundry detergent — 1 bottle
- Toothpaste — 1 tube
- Shampoo — 1 bottle

## Optional (Baby / Pet)
- Baby diapers / wipes (if applicable)
- Dog or cat food — 1 bag
