## General Rules

1. **Prefer primary sources**: Official blogs and press over third-party summaries when the story is an announcement.
2. **Prefer analysis over echo**: One in-depth or analytical source beats three rewrites of the same press release.
3. **Name the source on every item** in the digest so the user can trust and click through.
4. **If the user names a topic not listed** (e.g. "gaming", "climate tech"), use the closest category above and add 1–2 high-quality sources you know for that space; keep the bar high.
5. **For research papers**: Prefer arXiv, Semantic Scholar, Papers with Code, and OpenReview for ML/AI; PubMed for life sciences; ACM/IEEE for CS. Cite title, authors, venue/year, and link.
